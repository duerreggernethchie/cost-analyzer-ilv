Welcome to our Software Development Blogs

![](/cost-analyzer-ilv/images/se.png "https://github.com/fastai/fastpages")

With new technologies flooding every day, it is hard to keep up with all the updates. Blogs are a valuable resource for keeping up with recent innovations, communicating with fellow developers and getting a feel of what software development is all about.

This is where our motivation comes in. We have collected our software development portfolios aiming to walk you through different areas of Software Development in Data Science with the help of the concept implementation of the CSV-Insurance Cost Analyzer App. In this blog, we learn and understand software development terms, concept and their application into solving real world problems. We covered Requirements Engineering & Evolution, Software System & Architecture and Software Design. Thus, covering all the concept necessary to implementing an application or software.

The CSV-Cost Analyzer app aims to help user tracks his bank account's expenditures and receivables. The user will get an overview of all his expenses over several bank accounts by just uploading his bank CSV-data into the app. The tool is able to show a dashboard to a user with with an overview of all the clustered categories. The user is able to filter by date and categories as well. The scope and limitation of these portfolios are concept focused. For further understanding of the flow of the application, please proceed to [Requirements Engineering](https://duerreggernethchie.github.io/cost-analyzer-ilv/2020/07/10/Requirements-Engineering.html). or [Software System & Architecture](https://duerreggernethchie.github.io/cost-analyzer-ilv/2020/07/09/System-and-Software-Architecture.html)
